Mental Health Amenities

What are "safe zones"?

Safe zones are quiet areas designated for guests who may need a break from the festival atmosphere. These areas are designed to be low-stimulus and staffed with volunteers who can offer support and information about mental health resources.

How do I access a safe space?

Safe spaces are available to all attendees. They are marked on the festival map, and our staff can also guide you to the nearest location. No prior registration or permission is needed, and you can stay as long as you need.

Can I bring my medication?

Absolutely, we encourage you to bring any medications that you require. Please keep them in their original prescription bottles or packaging. If you need a place to store them or require refrigeration, please contact our medical staff when you arrive.

What should I do if I feel overwhelmed?

If you ever feel overwhelmed, we encourage you to visit one of our safe spaces where you can also seek out a mental health professional onsite. Our staff are trained to assist and can help you find the support you need. Remember, it's okay to ask for help.

Tickets

How do I purchase tickets for Safe & Sound?

Tickets can be purchased directly here on our official website. Please visit our tickets page for links to purchase and additional information.

Are there discounted tickets for people with disabilities or health conditions?

Yes, we offer discounted tickets for attendees with disabilities. We also provide free entry for one caretaker per attendee, if required. Please contact our ticket office for more information on how to apply for these tickets.
You can also check the accessibility section of this Q&A for more information on accessing the festival in the best conditions if you have a disability.

Is there a payment plan option?

Yes, we offer a payment plan to spread the cost of your ticket over several installments. Details about the payment plan and how to enroll are available on the ticket purchase page.

What should I do if I haven’t received my ticket confirmation?

Please check your email spam or junk folder first. If you still can’t find your confirmation, contact our customer support with your purchase details, and we will resend your confirmation.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else if I am unable to attend?

Yes, tickets are transferable up to 3 days before the festival starts. You can change the ticket holder's name through your ticket account on our website. Please ensure to provide the new attendee with the updated confirmation

What happens if the event is canceled or rescheduled?

In the event of cancellation, tickets will be fully refunded at the point of purchase. If the festival is rescheduled, tickets will remain valid for the new date. If you cannot attend on the rescheduled date, you can request a refund by contacting our support team.

Accessibility

What accessibility accommodations are available at Safe & Sound?

We are committed to making Safe & Sound accessible to everyone. Our accessibility accommodations include:

  • Wheelchair-accessible pathways and viewing areas.
  • Accessible restrooms throughout the festival grounds.
  • Sign language interpretation for all the performances.
  • Reserved parking close to the festival entrance for attendees with disabilities.

How do I request special accommodations?

If you require special accommodations, please contact our accessibility coordinator at name.surname@safeandsound.com at least 2 weeks before the festival. We will do our best to meet your needs and ensure you have a great experience at Safe & Sound.

Are service animals and emotionnal support animals allowed at the festival?

Yes, we welcome both service and emotionnal support animals providing you made a request with our accessibility coordinator at name.surname@safeandsound.com . If your request is accepted we will provide an animal pass specifying the reason for your animal's presence.
Having your animal with you will restrict a little your access to some zones of the festival as we want to make sure attendees having pet allergies are welcomed in the best conditions possible as well.
Please ensure that your animal is leashed and under control at all times.

Is there an area for sensory breaks

Yes, one of our 3 safe zones is actually conceived to accomodate people with sensory issues. This area is a quiet space with soft lighting, comfortable seating, and sensory-relaxation tools available. It is staffed by professionals trained to assist with sensory needs.

Cancellation Policy

Can I cancel my ticket at any time without a fee?

Yes, you can cancel your ticket at any time up to the event date without any cancellation fee. We understand that for us anxious people, it is hard to commit to a big event without knowing upon purchasing the tickets what state our mental health will be in that day and we don't want the financial investment to be a burden to any of our attendees.

How do I cancel my ticket?

To cancel your ticket, please log in to your account on our website and select the option to cancel your ticket. You will receive a confirmation of cancellation via email. If you encounter any issues, please contact our customer support team for assistance.

Will I get a full refund if I cancel my ticket?

Yes, if you cancel your ticket before the festival, you will receive a full refund, no questions asked. The refund will be processed to the original payment method used during purchase. Please allow up to 5 business days for the transaction to reflect in your account.

What happens if the festival is canceled or postponed?

If the festival is canceled, we will automatically issue full refunds to all ticket holders. If the festival is postponed, your ticket will be valid for the new date. Should you be unable to attend on the rescheduled date, you will have the option to request a full refund.

Is there a deadline for requesting a refund if the event is rescheduled?

Yes, if the event is rescheduled, you will have 30 days from the announcement to request a refund if you cannot attend on the new date.

Practical Infos

Are there designated smoking areas at the festival?

Yes, to ensure the comfort and safety of all our attendees, smoking (including e-cigarettes and vapes) is only allowed in designated smoking areas. These areas are clearly marked on the festival grounds map available on our website and at the event.

What is the alcohol policy at the festival?

  • Alcohol Sales: Alcohol is available for purchase at various bars throughout the festival grounds. You must be 21 years of age or older and provide valid photo ID to buy and consume alcohol.
  • Bringing Alcohol: To ensure safety and compliance with local laws, bringing your own alcohol to the festival is strictly prohibited. All bags will be checked at entry points.

What should I do in case of an emergency?

In case of an emergency, please locate the nearest staff member or proceed to one of our clearly marked Information or First Aid tents. We have medical and security staff on site who are trained to handle a variety of emergency situations. Also, familiarize yourself with the location of emergency exits and medical tents upon arrival.

Tips & tricks

Mind Your Wellness

  • Use Safe Spaces: If you ever feel overwhelmed, utilize the designated safe spaces for a break. They’re there to help you recharge in a quieter environment.
  • Pace Yourself: It’s a marathon, not a sprint. Enjoy the festival atmosphere without overexerting yourself early in the day.

Be Mindful of Others

  • Consent and Personal Space: Always respect personal boundaries and ask for consent if you're interacting in ways that might affect someone else.
  • Respect the Space: Keep the festival clean by using trash and recycling bins. Be mindful of your environment and the people around you.

Stay Connected

  • Designate Meetup Points: Mobile service can be spotty at festivals, so set up specific times and places to meet with friends if you get separated.
  • Portable Chargers: Keep your phone charged for photos, schedules, and in case you need to get in touch with someone. There will be charging stations but we cannot guarantee they will be able to accomodate everyone's need so come prepared.

Stay Hydrated and Nourished

  • Hydration Stations: Make use of hydration stations if available. Staying hydrated is key, especially if it’s warm or you’re dancing a lot.
  • Food Options: Explore different food vendors to keep your energy up throughout the day. Eating regular meals or snacks can prevent dips in energy and help regulate emotions and anxiety.
